## Required Images in `images` Folder

Place the following images in your `images` folder:

### **Campus Gallery Photos (6 images):**

1. **campus1.jpg** - Main Building

   - Front view preferred
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px

2. **campus2.jpg** - Zoology Department
   - Department building/block
   - Clear view of the department entrance
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px

3. **campus3.jpg** - Laboratory
   - Modern lab facilities
   - Students working in lab (optional)
   - Equipment visible
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px

4. **campus4.jpg** - Library
   - Library interior or exterior
   - Bookshelves, reading area
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px

5. **campus5.jpg** - Classroom
   - Classroom with desks/chairs
   - Teaching environment
   - Students in class (optional)
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px

6. **campus6.jpg** - Campus Ground
   - Sports ground or open area
   - Green spaces
   - Students activities (optional)
   - Recommended size: 1200x800px
